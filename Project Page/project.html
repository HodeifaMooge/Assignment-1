Claybrook Zoo

At Claybrook Zoo, I was a part of the Records Management System team, which was responsible for designing and producing the records management system. We are responsible for the state of the RMS at Claybrook Zoo, keeping it up to date with the system, and making changes if necessary to deal with all of the records, such as animal records, medical records etc.
